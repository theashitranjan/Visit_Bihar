Other Famous Destinaion

Tomb of Sher Shah Suri

Sher Shah Suri's tomb, located in Sasaram, Bihar, is a grand 16th-century Indo-Islamic architectural masterpiece built in red sandstone, standing in the middle of an artificial lake as a tribute to the Afghan ruler.

Kanwar Lake

Kanwar Lake, located in Begusarai, Bihar, is Asia's largest freshwater oxbow lake and a vital wetland ecosystem, serving as a habitat for numerous migratory birds.

Manjhar Kund

Manjhar Kund and Dhuan Kund in Sasaram, Bihar, are scenic waterfalls known for their natural beauty, refreshing ambiance, and historical significance, attracting tourists and pilgrims alike.

Umga Temple

Umga Temple, located in the Aurangabad district of Bihar, is an ancient Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu, renowned for its intricate stone carvings and architectural resemblance to the Sun Temple at Konark.
